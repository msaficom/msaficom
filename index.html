INSTALLATION

The company has a technical team which installs the system for our customers.

Since time immemorial,Kenya has been experiencing many and fatal accidents oftenly caused by overloading.Though we have ways that have been put in place to curb with overloading such as legislative means and weighing bridge we still have damaged roads,overloading related accidents and cases of overloading in vehicles.We thus concluded that it was better to come with a portable gadget that could be installed in vehicles.If a vehicle owner tries to overload the engine automatically goes off and an sms is sent to our company to show the attempt.if a driver tries to tamper with the gadget an sms is also sent to inform the company about it
